# Sword & Shield Mega Redux

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-blue)](https://github.com/lincoln-lm/swsh-mega-redux/releases)
[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Asset_Repository-purple)](https://github.com/lincoln-lm/swsh-mega-redux-romfs)

**swsh-mega-redux** is a work-in-progress mod that aims to bring Mega Evolution to Sword & Shield.

<!-- video -->

## Installation

### Atmosphère

1. Download the release
1. Unzip to your sd card such that the ``atmosphere`` folder merges with the one on your sd card
1. You should now have the following folders on your sd card:
    - Shield:
        - ``sd:/atmosphere/contents/01008DB008C2C000/exefs/``
        - ``sd:/atmosphere/contents/01008DB008C2C000/romfs/``
    - Sword:
        - ``sd:/atmosphere/contents/0100ABF008968000/exefs/``
        - ``sd:/atmosphere/contents/0100ABF008968000/romfs/``
